Earthquake

An earthquake is a sudden and powerful shaking of the Earth's surface caused by the movement of tectonic plates beneath the Earth's crust, often resulting in damage to buildings and landscapes.

Typhoon

Typhoon winds can be strong enough to damage or destroy homes, businesses, and other structures. This can include damage to roofs, walls, windows, and doors. Typhoons can also damage or destroy roads, bridges, and other infrastructure.
